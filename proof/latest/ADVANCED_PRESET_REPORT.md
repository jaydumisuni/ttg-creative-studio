# Advanced Mode Preset Report

These presets define the professional controls that must be exposed through Advanced Mode.

## Text Material

- **Neon Chrome** (`neon_chrome`): Purple/cyan gradient with glow, stroke and extrusion.
- **Glass White** (`glass_white`): Clean white glass text with soft blue glow.
- **Deep Purple 3D** (`deep_purple_3d`): Darker 3D purple face with strong extrusion.

## Effects

- **Premium Wordmark Stack** (`premium_wordmark`): Full wordmark polish stack.
- **Glass Card Stack** (`glass_card`): Glass panel with outline, blur-look fill, glow and reflection.

## Scene

- **Cyber Floor** (`cyber_floor`): Perspective grid with foreground sweep lines.

## Motion

- **Dark Start Reveal** (`dark_start_reveal`): Start black, then light beams and logo come alive.
- **Side Light Wipe** (`side_light_wipe`): Purple/cyan side panels sweep into frame.
- **Final Glow Lock** (`final_glow_lock`): Final bloom pulse and clean lockup.

## Export

- **YouTube Intro 1080p** (`youtube_intro_1080p`): 1920x1080, 30fps, H.264 proof target.
- **Social Preview GIF** (`social_preview_gif`): Small animated preview for review.

## Gate

The hidden proof renderer is not enough. These presets must become visible/editable in the app UI before release.
